# Cool Barcode Games

## Overview
Grab your barcode scanner and some items to play. Scan items in real life and play in lots of different mini games.

[(oh and if you don't have a barcode scanner, here's a nice video)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=bVPGMyppgw4)

## Mini Games
* Barcodemania
  * Scan all the items you see on screen before the time runs out. Don't scan the wrong item, or you will lose time faster.

* Hot Barcode
  * Play against your friend to find the item with the biggest barcode (digits summed).

* Rhythm Superstore
  * Scan the items as they come along the conveyor belt. Try to scan them all before they get to the end.

* Barcodle
  * Wordle but with barcodes.
